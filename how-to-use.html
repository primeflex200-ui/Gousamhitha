üõí How to Shop

Step-by-Step Shopping Guide

  • 1 Create an Account
    Click "Login" in the top navigation, then select "Sign Up" to create your account. You'll need your email and mobile number.
  • 2 Browse Products
    Explore our categories from the menu or use the search bar to find specific products. Each product shows vendor information and availability.
  • 3 View Product Details
    Click on any product to see detailed information, including vendor name, business details, price, and stock availability.
  • 4 Add to Cart
    Click "Add to Cart" on products you want to purchase. You can buy from multiple vendors in one order!
  • 5 Review Your Cart
    Click the "Cart" icon to review your items, adjust quantities, or remove products.
  • 6 Checkout
    Proceed to checkout, enter your delivery address, and complete your payment.
  • 7 Track Your Order
    Go to "My Orders" to track your delivery status and view order history.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order from multiple vendors at once?

Yes! You can add products from different vendors to your cart and checkout together. Each vendor will process and deliver their items separately.

How do I know which vendor is selling a product?

Every product card displays the vendor name and business name clearly. You can see this information before adding items to your cart.

What if a product is out of stock?

Out of stock products will show an "Out of Stock" badge and cannot be added to cart. Check back later or contact the vendor for restock information.

How do I track my order?

Go to "My Orders" in the navigation menu. You'll see all your orders with current status and tracking information.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel orders before they are shipped. Go to "My Orders" and select the order you want to cancel.

What is A2 Ghee?

A2 Ghee is made from milk of desi cows that produce A2 beta-casein protein. It's considered healthier and easier to digest than regular ghee.
